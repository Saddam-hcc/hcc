What is the internet

Definition of Internet

The Internet is a huge global network that connects billions of computers and phones to each other around the world. You can imagine it as a "network of networks" that allows the exchange of data, information and files between any two devices connected to it.

How it works

  1. Communication: The devices are connected via undersea, satellite, or wireless cables.

  2. Protocols are the languages used by devices for understanding, the most famous of which is HTTP (used in browsing your current site).

  3. Address: Every device or website has a unique address called IP Address, which allows the data to reach its correct destination.

The difference between "Internet" and "Web":

The Internet is the "infrastructure" and the wires and devices that connect the world.

Web (World Wide Web): It is the "service" that we use to browse websites and it works over the Internet.
